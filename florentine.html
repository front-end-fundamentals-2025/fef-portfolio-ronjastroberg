FLORENTINE

MODERN

While the Italian Cuisine is traditional, the idea behind this identity was to embrace traditionality yet giving it modern twist, making it fit in with the Scandinavian more modern and minimalistic style. Combining the traditional Florence elements in the logo together contemporary patterns and illustrations created a unique vibe.

MEMORABLE

The idea was to create a memorable identity and logo that make people remember the place and the feelings they experienced while being there. By mixing traditional and playful elements I hope to deliver a setting and a feeling that people will talk about and want to come back to. Scandinavian style merged with Italian cooking is the best of both worlds and those worlds are capsuled in this restaurant.

PLAYFUL

To create a unique visual identity I added colors that reminds me of when I lived in Florence. All inspiration for this project comes from my own experience and I wanted to bring the happy feeling I got there back to Sweden and the identity of this restaurant. Adding fun colors and illustrations gives the identity a depth and gives the restaurant an personality.
